The Analyst Behind the Data

I'm Ezeani D. Osita, a Data Analyst and Business Intelligence Developer based in Lagos, Nigeria. I specialize in transforming messy, raw datasets into clean, compelling dashboards and reports that drive real decisions.

My background spans retail operations, energy sector inspections, and education — giving me a multi-sector lens for data problems. I've honed my craft through industry bootcamps (UTIVA Academy), NCDMB/HAAC Energy BI certifications, and virtual hackathons.
